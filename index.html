Our Story

A place to unwind & savor

Nestled in the heart of Smyrna, Georgia, Indigo Room exudes warmth and hospitality, welcoming guests with open arms. With a cozy atmosphere, friendly staff, and a menu filled with mouthwatering dishes curated by Executive Chef Winston Gordon.

Our diverse offerings cater to every palate — whether you're enjoying a leisurely dinner with loved ones, meeting friends for a quick lunch, or relaxing with a solo bite at the bar. Come as you are, and leave with a satisfied heart, a full stomach, and a smile.

Our intimate stage features live jazz and neo-soul performances weekly, adding the perfect soundtrack to your experience.
